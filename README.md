# TradeDex

TradeDex is a modern Inventory Management System (IMS) and Point of Sale (POS) application built for Pokémon Trading Card Game (TCG) businesses.

## Goals

- Manage raw and graded Pokémon cards
- Track inventory
- Record purchases and sales
- Manage customer transactions
- Track profit and loss
- Support sealed products and accessories

## Core Features

### Inventory Management

- Add Pokémon cards
- Inventory adjustments
- Card variants
- Graded cards
- Condition tracking

### Sales

- POS
- Barcode scanning
- Discounts
- Receipts
- Customer history

### Purchasing

- Supplier management
- Purchase orders
- Cost tracking

### Analytics

- Inventory valuation
- Sales reports
- Profit reports
- Best-selling cards
- Stock alerts
